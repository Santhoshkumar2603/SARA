    Hand Eye Coordination Test

    Hand-eye coordination is the coordinated control of eye movement with hand reaction. We’ve SARA designed and developed a mini-game for you to test your hand-eye coordination abilities. In each round, you will see two balls, an axis, and a white dashed circle. The balls will move along the axis, and your goal is to get the moving balls to stop within the white dashed circle. The balls stop moving when you click/tap. The white circle turns green if you get both balls to stop within the circle and yellow if you get one to stop within the circle. If both balls stop outside of the circle, the circle turns red.

    This test consists of 5 rounds starting from easy to hard. You need to complete all 5 rounds to ace the test.
